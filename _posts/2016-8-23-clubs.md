## Barming Youth FC

<p><span class="image left"><img src="/forty-jekyll-theme/assets/images/clubs/Barming Youth FC.png" alt="" /></span></p>

Barming Youth F.C was founded in 1980 with just one team. The club has grown extensively since then, with over 30 teams including girls, ladies, men's and veterans teams. The aim of the club is to provide all local youth, opportunities to play football regardless of their abilities.The club has become an F.A. Charter Standard club. The Charter Standard Scheme is a best practice guide that sets standards of coaching, administration and child protection for clubs working with young people. As a benchmark for quality, it will improve the playing experience for all.

Barming Youth has 32 teams this season from Under 5s up to Seniors, including three girls sides, and these teams play at Barming School, South Street, Barming Heath, Poplar Grove, Mereworth, Gatland Lane, Eccles and West Malling.

## Castle Colts FC

<p><span class="image left"><img src="/forty-jekyll-theme/assets/images/clubs/Castle Colts FC.png" alt="" /></span></p>

Castle Colts Football Club was established in 1970 and is proud to have achieved the FA Charter Standard Mark that incorporates First Aid and Child Protection requirements. We provide footballing opportunities for Boys and Girls from the age of 5 to 18. In recent years we have also established successful Mens and Womens senior teams.

We are associated with Allington Community Association (ACA) using facilities at the Orchard Ground and Allington Primary School. As a result all club members get membership to the ACA as part of their Castle Colts subscription. This ensures that we as a club can provide a chance for our teams to play league football in a friendly, safe and fun environment.

Castle Colts is the official football club for the Allington area. As well as the ongoing support from the Allington Community Association, it also has wide support from local business and from the parents of children that attend all age groups of the club.
