## Anchorians FC

<p><span class="image left"><img src="/forty-jekyll-theme/assets/images/clubs/Anchorians FC.png" alt="" /></span></p>

Anchorians Football Club a FA Charter Standard Community club, based in Gillingham we provide football for boys, girls, men, ladies & disabilities culminating in over 600 members, some 54 teams that participate each week across the South East of England.
 
Anchorians Football club has steadily grown over the past 4-5 years and we were proud to receive the award of Medway Junior Sports Club of 2014, 2015 & 2016 and FA Charter Standard Community Club of the Year for Kent 2015, an award which we were also awarded in 2013.

## Barming Youth FC

<p><span class="image left"><img src="/forty-jekyll-theme/assets/images/clubs/Barming Youth FC.png" alt="" /></span></p>

Barming Youth F.C was founded in 1980 with just one team. The club has grown extensively since then, with over 30 teams including girls, ladies, men's and veterans teams. The aim of the club is to provide all local youth, opportunities to play football regardless of their abilities.The club has become an F.A. Charter Standard club. The Charter Standard Scheme is a best practice guide that sets standards of coaching, administration and child protection for clubs working with young people. As a benchmark for quality, it will improve the playing experience for all.

Barming Youth has 32 teams this season from Under 5s up to Seniors, including three girls sides, and these teams play at Barming School, South Street, Barming Heath, Poplar Grove, Mereworth, Gatland Lane, Eccles and West Malling.

## Castle Colts FC

<p><span class="image left"><img src="/forty-jekyll-theme/assets/images/clubs/Castle Colts FC.png" alt="" /></span></p>

Castle Colts Football Club was established in 1970 and is proud to have achieved the FA Charter Standard Mark that incorporates First Aid and Child Protection requirements. We provide footballing opportunities for Boys and Girls from the age of 5 to 18. In recent years we have also established successful Mens and Womens senior teams.

We are associated with Allington Community Association (ACA) using facilities at the Orchard Ground and Allington Primary School. As a result all club members get membership to the ACA as part of their Castle Colts subscription. This ensures that we as a club can provide a chance for our teams to play league football in a friendly, safe and fun environment.

Castle Colts is the official football club for the Allington area. As well as the ongoing support from the Allington Community Association, it also has wide support from local business and from the parents of children that attend all age groups of the club.

## Kings Hill FC

<p><span class="image left"><img src="/forty-jekyll-theme/assets/images/clubs/Kings Hill FC.png" alt="" /></span></p>

Kings Hill FC was established in 2003 and provides grass-roots community football for over 300 players from U6's through to Veterans. The club has expanded rapidly over the last 10 years and our ambitions have grown to match.

In 2013 we moved to our new home ground at the Kings Hill Sports Park. With 5 professionally laid and maintained grass pitches as well as a floodlit 3G along with a Pavilion with changing rooms, bar and social area these facilities are the basis of our ambition over the coming years.  We are fortunate enough to have the use of some of the best facilities for a grass-roots club anywhere in Kent. Kings Hill Sports Park is also home to Rondos academy. We will continue to work closely with these expert coaching providers to maximise quality of coaching content provided to our players.

Whether you live on Kings Hill or not, you will be welcomed at Kings Hill FC. Our Elite squads accept the best players available whilst our Colts and Development squads are more community focused. We are an inclusive club that will provide football for all ages, abilities and genders where required. We now have 2 dedicated girls teams, and a girls development center that runs every Saturday morning.

#### Teams entered

 **U10:** Kings Hill Rangers FC **U15:** Kings Hill Colts Under 15 A, Kings Hill Colts Under 15 B
